Federal Minister for Energy (Power Division), Sardar Awais Ahmad Khan Leghari, chaired a consultative meeting with all Distribution Companies (DISCOs) on the transition from feeder-level to transformer-level load management.
This transformational initiative aims to enable more targeted identification of high-loss consumers, improve energy accounting, enhance operational visibility, and support equitable load management through data-driven decision-making.
Power Planning and Monitoring Company (PPMC), as the technical arm of the Ministry of Energy (Power Division), is playing a central role in supporting the planning, design, and effective implementation of this sector-wide reform. The Asset Performance Management System (APMS) will serve as a key enabler in achieving these objectives through transformer-level monitoring, analytics, digital oversight and enforcement mechanisms across DISCOs.
