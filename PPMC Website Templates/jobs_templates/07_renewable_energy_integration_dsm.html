Renewable Energy Integration and DSM through advanced digital Infrastructure

Responsibilities

Provide technical input for integrating DERs (e.g., solar, storage, EVs) into the distribution grid in line with energy transition goals
Conduct assessments of AMI, SCADA, EMS, and APMS to improve grid control, monitoring, and operational efficiency
Contribute to the development of policy and regulatory frameworks for energy efficiency and DSM implementation
Advise on cybersecurity and data governance for digital infrastructure to ensure system integrity and compliance
Design and deliver training programs to enhance capacity of PPMC staff and DISCOs on DERs, DSM, and digitalization
Prepare technical reports, policy drafts, operational guidelines, and data models to support project objectives
Coordinate with planners, utilities, and regulators to ensure data-driven, future-ready infrastructure decisions
