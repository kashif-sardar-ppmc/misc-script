Transmission & Distribution Infrastructure Planning

Responsibilities

Evaluate transmission and distribution (T&D) expansion and reinforcement plans in line with demand growth and renewable integration
Conduct power system studies, including load flow, stability, and contingency analysis to ensure grid security and reliability
Develop and maintain T&D system models and run scenario studies using tools like PSS®E, DIgSILENT, or ETAP
Assess technical and economic performance of existing T&D infrastructure and recommend investment priorities
Analyze ancillary service needs and propose solutions involving batteries, STATCOMs, synthetic inertia, and synchronous condensers
Develop tools and methodologies for planning flexible grid operations under high renewable energy penetration
Review and advise on updates to grid codes and planning criteria to support evolving system requirements
